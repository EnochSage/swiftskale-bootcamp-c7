This fully virtual Data Analytics Internship Bootcamp is designed to introduce learners to practical data analytics concepts, tools, workflows, and project-based learning. All training sessions will be recorded and made accessible to participants to support flexible and self-paced learning.

Participants are expected to attend sessions where possible, complete assignments, submit tasks on time, and actively participate in training activities in order to stay on track and successfully complete the program.
